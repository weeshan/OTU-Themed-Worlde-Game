How to Play Wordle Classic

Welcome to Wordle Classic mode! Here's how to play:

  • Word length: 5 letters
  • You have 5 attempts to guess the word.
  • Blue: Correct letter in the correct position.
  • Orange: Correct letter, wrong position.
  • Gray: Letter not in the word.

Use the on-screen keyboard or your device keyboard to play.

Press Enter to submit your guess or Backspace to edit your input.

Good luck!
