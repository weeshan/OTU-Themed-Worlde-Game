How to Play Wordle Blitz

Answer as fast as you can before the timer runs out!

  • 4 letters per word, and 6 attempts to guess the word!
  • Letters highlighted in blue: correct position.
  • Letters highlighted in orange: correct letter, wrong position.
  • Letters highlighted in gray: incorrect letter.

Use the on-screen keyboard or your device keyboard to play. When ready, press Enter to submit your guess or Backspace to edit your input.

Good luck!
