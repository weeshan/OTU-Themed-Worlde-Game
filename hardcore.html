How to Play Wordle Hardcore

Ready for a challenge?

  • 7 letters per word, and 5 attempts to guess the word!
  • orange: letter exists in the word, but its position is not revealed.
  • gray: letter does not exist in the word.
  • No clues are given bout the correct positioning of the letters — it's up to you to figure them out!

Use the on-screen keyboard or your device keyboard to play. Press Enter to submit your guess or Backspace to edit your input.

Good luck!
