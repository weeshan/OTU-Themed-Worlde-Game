How to Play

Wordle is a fun and challenging word game! The goal is to guess the secret word within the given number of attempts. Here's how it works:

  • Type in your guess using the on-screen keyboard or your own.
  • Each letter in your guess will be highlighted based on its correctness:
    • Blue: The letter is in the correct position.
    • Orange: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
    • Gray: The letter is not in the word.
  • You have a limited number of attempts to guess the word. Choose your letters wisely!
  • Game modes like Blitz, Hardcore, and Classic offer different challenges.

Good luck, and have fun!
